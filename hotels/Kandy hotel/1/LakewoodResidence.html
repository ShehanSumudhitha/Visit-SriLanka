Lakewood Residence
Kandy

Situated in Kandy, a 3-minute walk from Kandy City Center Shopping Mall, Lakewood Residence features free bikes and an outdoor swimming pool, as well as free WiFi. The property has a terrace, as well as a shared lounge. Bogambara Stadium is 300 m from the property.

At the hotel, all rooms have a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, and certain rooms are equipped with a balcony. The rooms will provide guests with a wardrobe and a kettle. Guests at Lakewood Residence can enjoy a continental breakfast. At the accommodation you will find a restaurant serving Thai and Chinese cuisine. Vegetarian and halal options can also be requested. Guests can make use of the indoor pool.
