Oliphant Boutique Villa by Amaya
Nuwara Eliya

Featuring a restaurant, a shared lounge, and a garden, Oliphant Boutique Villa by Amaya provides accommodation in Nuwara Eliya with free WiFi and garden views. Boasting free private parking, the villa is in an area where guests can engage in activities such as hiking and darts. This villa comes with a kitchenette, a seating area, a dining area and a cable flat-screen TV. Guests at the villa can enjoy a continental breakfast.
