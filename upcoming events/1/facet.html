The Facets Show
Nuwara Eliya

Held annually in Colombo since 1991 with state and private sector patronage, FACETS Sri Lanka 2020, the 30th International Gem & Jewellery Show will have hundreds of local and foreign exhibitors showcasing their gem and jewellery products and a large number of influential trade visitors from around the world, which promises a "Bigger & Better" FACETS Sri Lanka 2020 than ever before. Whether you are a buyer, a visitor or an exhibitor, this is the one event you should put down on your diaries.

The Venue

quipped with ultra-modern facilities, The BMICH EXHIBITION AND CONVENTION CENTRE in Colombo, is an internationally acclaimed exhibition venue perfect for a smart, exciting gem show. Located on beautiful sprawling gardens within Colombo City, just a few minutes drive from the main city hotels, the venue has two separate halls and a bright, spacious lobby. Gemstone wholesalers are grouped in one hall while jewellery and other services are in a separate hall.
